St Johns Beatmaking School is Portland's most dynamic beatmaking school, offering students of all ages and abilities the opportunity to become the best musicians/producers they can be. Our mission is to not just teach the software, but also how to speak the creative language of music. We are dedicated to preparing our students for a lifetime of music enjoyment that extends well beyond their years as a student. Whether your main love is hip hop, EDM, experimental, pop or beyond, our school has a specialized program for you.
  • Learn Ableton 9 and other music software in a state of the art computer lab
  • Learn to use midi controllers and hardware
  • Become fluent in the language of music
  • Connect with peers in the local beatmaking community
  • Individual and group lessons available
  • Monthly group demos and show and tell sessions
  • Lessons on traditional instruments (guitar, piano, drums, etc.) available
  • Affordable lessons
  • Scholarships available
