St Johns Beatmaking School is Portland's most dynamic beatmaking school, offering students of all ages and abilities the opportunity to become the best musicians/producers they can be. Our mission is to not just teach the software, but also how to speak the creative language of music. We are dedicated to preparing our students for a lifetime of music enjoyment that extends well beyond their years as a student. Whether your main love is hip hop, EDM, pop, experimental, or beyond, our school has a specialized program for you.
